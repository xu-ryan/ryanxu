about

The act of writing has always been an art. Now, it can also be an act of music. Each letter you type corresponds to a specific musical note putting a new spin to your composition. Personalize your writing by choosing between six unique moods. Each mood changes speed, filter and color to each letter's musical note. Easily import text written in other writing applications with a copy and paste interface. When you've finished writing, share it and download an audio version with a click of a button! Whether it's a message, essay, story, or poem explore a new way of writing.
